Ingredients

This tofu scramble is easy as 1, 2, 3! Gluten-free and delicious, make it for yourself or to share!

  • 12 to 14-ounce block extra firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Heaping ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Heaping ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin
