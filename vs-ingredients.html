Ingredients

This gluten-free vanilla protein smoothie is so refreshing. Easy to make and easy to share!

  • 1 cup frozen pineapple
  • 1 banana (room temperature)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup milk (or almond milk or oat milk)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 ice cubes
  • Garnish with oasted coconut and fresh raspberries
