Ingredients

Cinnamon Raisin Bread is perfect for any occasion. Delicious, filling, and full of nutrients!

  • 2 cups plus 1 tablespoon gluten-free all-purpose baking flour (without xanthan gum)
  • 1 cup sugar, divided
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • Dairy-free spreadable margarine, optional
