Ingredients

This is a delicious and versatile dish. Perfect for gatherings!

  • 1lb pork sausage
  • 1 large shallot or 1 small onion, chopped
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 8oz sour cream
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 8oz shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
  • 4 cups (about 20oz,) frozen shredded hash browns, thawed
