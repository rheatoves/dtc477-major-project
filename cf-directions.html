Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients for the marinate together in a large ziploc bag or shallow dish. Add the sliced chicken and mix to coat it evenly. Marinate for at least 1 hour or up to 12 hours.
  2. Heat a large skillet with 1 tablespoon of oil. Add the chicken and all the marinade to the skillet and saute for 3-4 minutes, bringing the sauce to a full boil.
  3. Add the sliced onions, peppers, and tomatoes and cook until they are just tender, another 5-7 minutes.
  4. Serve on corn tortillas with your favorite toppings such as avocado, feta cheese (or queso fresco), and salsa. Enjoy!
