Directions

  1. Heat a large dutch oven or pot over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil. Add the meat and cook until browned, breaking up the beef and sausage with a spatula (or meat chopper) as you stir.
  2. Add the onion, green pepper and jalapeno and cook for 3 more minutes or until softened.
  3. Add the chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper, brown sugar, bay leaves, garlic and tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, chicken stock and beans and stir to combine. Bring to a simmer then reduce the heat and cook, stirring often for 40-50 minutes. Let sit with the lid on and the heat off for 10 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
