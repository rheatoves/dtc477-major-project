Directions

  1. Drain the tofu container, then pat the tofu dry with a clean dish towel.
  2. Place the tofu in a large bowl and use your fingers to break the tofu into bite-sized irregular shaped pieces.
  3. Stir in the olive oil, turmeric, salt and pepper.
  4. Add the cumin and garlic powder to a dry skillet.
  5. Cook on medium heat for 1 minute until fragrant. Stir in the tofu and increase the heat to medium high. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through and starting to lightly brown.
  6. Remove the heat and place it on the serving plates. Serve with toast, veggie saute (below), or if you want to get fancy, vegan bacon.
